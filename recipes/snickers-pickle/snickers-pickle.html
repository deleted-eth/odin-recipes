Snickers-Pickle, or "The Snickle"

Description

The Snickers-pickle, perhaps better known by its street name, the "snickle", is the stuff of a 'Lovecraftian' nightmare. An eldritch horror beyond earthly comprehension. Last summer, when my family and I visted Egypt, we did a tour of the ancient ruins which was way cooler than last summer's visit to Grandpa Paul in hospice. Anyways, as we were leaving one of the temples I was struck by an etching. At the time I didn't know what to make of it. I've studied my fair share of hieroglyphs and let's just say I'd never seen one like this. Before I was pulled away, I managed to snap a picture. I couldn't find it before typing up this blog, but trust me it looked just like the photo I included above. The Egyptians must have made contact with some alien force that showed them things from the future, I can't explain it any other way. If you go to Egypt keep your eye out. I think there's more to them as a society than we're taught in school.

Credit to Punky-LookingKiddo on Reddit.com.

Ingredients

  • 1 large pickle (homemade or store bought)
  • 1 Snickers bar

Steps

  1. Use a knife to cut the pickle in half
  2. Carefully carve out a cavity roughly the size of a Snickers bar
  3. Wedge the Snicker bar into one half of the pickle (trim where necessary)
  4. Sheath the remainder of the Snickers bar with the other half of the pickle
  5. Enjoy
