Ingredients

  • 6 frozen, wild caught jumbo shrimp
  • All natural peanut butter
  • Reduced sugar jelly or jam (any flavor)

Steps

  1. Thaw shrimp in room temp water for 15-20 minutes
  2. Cook shrimp in a pan for about 3 minutes on each side
  3. Shovel a heaping portion of peanut butter on top of the shrimp
  4. Drizzle jam or jelly over the shrimp, to taste
