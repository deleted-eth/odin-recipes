Pineapple Pizza

Description

The pineapple pizza is perhaps the most notorious of the food crimes. I first encountered the barbeque-laced, sour circle in high school, sitting around a campfire. Far along the path behind the Murphys' house, on a hard-pat beach shining a dull silver in the moonlight, my friend John shared stories of a vile pie in a hushed tone. The wind off the desolate gulf coast rattled the empty branches. Despite his obvious embellishments, I remember the cold working its way into my bones. I was sick for days after that night. Fifteen years would pass before I could muster the strength to open a can of pineapple chunks.

This recipe I found online is super easy to make. All credit goes to Melanie at Melaniecooks.com.

Ingredients

  • 1 large pizza crust (store bought or homemade)
  • 1 can 8oz pineapple chunks (or fresh cut-up pineapple)
  • 1 small onion sliced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Spread the tomato sauce over the pizza crust
  3. Sprinkle with shredded cheese
  4. Put onion slices and pineapple chunks on top
  5. Bake for 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted
